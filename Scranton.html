Scranton, Pennsylvania!

Some Scranton facts:

  • The population of Scranton is 76,328
  • In 1856, Scranton was incorporated as a bourough and became a city in 1866.
  • It is located in the north east part of the state.
  • It is an urban city.
  • Scranton has an average income of $23,103 USD compared to the state average of $32,156 USD.
Scrantons nickname used to be "Electric City" after the Electric lights were introduced in 1880.
