Philadelphia, Pennsylvania!

Philadelphia is the largest city in the state.

  • As the nations sixth largest city, the population of Philadelphia is 1,603,797.
  • Philadelphia was founded in 1691 by William Penn.
  • It is located in the south east part of the state.
  • It is an urban city.
  • Philadelphia has an average income of $27,331 USD compared to the state average of $32,156 USD.
Philadelphia was the states first capital.
