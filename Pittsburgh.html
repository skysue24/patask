Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania!

Pittsburgh is one of the largest cities in Pennsylvania. Here is a list of some of the most important facts about Pittsburgh:

  • Pittsburghs population is 300,431.
  • It was founded in 1751 as a fort and built up to a city since then.
  • It is located in the south western part of the state.
  • It is a urban city.
  • The average income for Pittsburgh is $29,399 USD compared to the state average of $32,156 USD.
A fun fact about Pittsburgh is that in Pittsburgh, it rains/snows more than it does in Seattle!
