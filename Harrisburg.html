Harrisburg, Pennsylvania!

Harrisburg is the Capital of Pennsylvania. It is home to over 200 years worth of history!

  • Harrisburg has a population of 591,712
  • It was founded in 1791 after being home to Native American tribes for thousands of years..
  • It is located in the south central part of the state.
  • It is an urban city.
  • Harrisburg has an average income of $25,826 USD compared to the state average of $32,156 USD.
Harrisburg had a huge part in the American civil war. It served as a major training camp and had many railways.
